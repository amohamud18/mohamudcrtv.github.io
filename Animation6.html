Meet the Designer - Action Figure

The aim of this project was to create a fun way to introduce myself as a designer and to capitalize on the AI Action Figure trend that has been present on Social Media without using any AI.

I was inspired by seeing many posts of people making themselves into action figures but I was equally disappointed by the use of AI in this case. It seemed like such a fun way to represent a person that I really wanted to give it a shot!

Programs used for this project: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator, Blender
