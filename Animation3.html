Logo Animation Project

The aim of this project was to animate a classic logo, I chose the Couvoir Dorval Logo. The logo chosen for the assignment is the Couvoir Dorval logo. Founded in 1977, Couvoir Dorval was one of Canadaâ€™s foremost turkey hatcheries. The logo was designed by designer Glenn Fretz.

The logo is a simple icon depicting a chick within an egg. The outer circle around the chick is divided in the middle, and the left and right sides show a C and a D respectively. The chick conveys a lot of character with its simple rendering, so for my animation, I wished to take advantage of that.
