About Me

I'm a Somali illustrator and designer based in Edmonton. I am passionate about bringing characters to life and expressing my creativity through playful designs. Inspired by the worlds of animation, video games, and retro aesthetics, I infuse my work with a unique charm.

My strengths lie in Illustration and branding. Through my work, I express myself in every project, aiming to craft stylized designs and characters that spark joy and inspiration. It's all about channelling my creativity into playful designs and captivating illustrations that resonate with people.
