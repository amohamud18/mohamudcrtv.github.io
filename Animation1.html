Project: History of Retro Gaming

For this project, I chose to do a documentary series about the history of retro gaming, beginning with the arcade era in the 1980s. The animation features minimalist, vector-based graphics reminiscent of early arcade games.

This animation and its concept was born out of my appreciation for video games as a whole and this animation celebrates the history of retro gaming.
