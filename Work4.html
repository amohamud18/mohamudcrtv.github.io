IslamicFamily - Law Made Simple

Link to Website

This project is a legal guide by IslamicFamily designed to support refugees, immigrants and racialized communities in Alberta by helping them understand their rights and responsibilities under Canadian law. This legal guide focuses on the legal issues newcomers to Canada may face. from immigration sponsorship and divorce, to family violence, parenting, and support. My role in this project was to create a visual style guide for this project, as well as create the visual content as well. Which include multiple illustrations as well as 3 animated educational videos.
