IslamicFamily - Law Made Simple

Link to Website

This project is a comprehensive legal guide developed by IslamicFamily to support refugees, immigrants, and racialized communities in Alberta. It aims to help newcomers understand their rights and responsibilities under Canadian law, addressing key topics such as immigration sponsorship, divorce, family violence, parenting, and support.

My role involved establishing the project's visual style and producing all creative assets. This included a cohesive illustration system and a series of three animated educational videos, designed to make complex legal information accessible, culturally sensitive, and engaging for diverse audiences.
